~ Save the Date ~

Festivities begin at sunset on Friday, September 11th 2026 and end at sunset on Sunday the 13th — all patrons are free to stay on site until Monday morning to ensure a rested drive home.

~ The Land ~

This gathering takes place at the foothills of protected ancestral forest, on traditional unceded territory of the Algonquin, Huron-Wendat, and Anishinabewaki Nations. It is today an expansive nature cooperative in the Laurentians 1.5 ~ 2hrs north of ‘Montréal’.

~ The Site ~

Our venue for the weekend is an exclusive lakeside site with no neighbors - complete with sauna, kayaks, hiking trails & unlimited stargazing included in the experience. Shoutout to our friends at Bohême Système for being the first to imagine a gathering here and for introducing us to this beautiful playground.

~ The Vision ~

Murmur is shaped as much by participation as programming: come to contribute, not to consume. You are invited to bring your strange ideas to life, to share skill-sets, to let your freak flag fly, or to simply volunteer to help make this project possible. More information on that soon.

~ Accommodation ~

All Murmites stay on-site; most tickets include accommodation in shared dorms or chalets. Reserve a cabin with friends or arrive solo and make new friends along the way. Camping options will be available.
